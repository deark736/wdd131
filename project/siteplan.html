Personal Travel Blog Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Wanderlust Chronicles

Reason: This name encapsulates the spirit of adventure and exploration that defines the travel blog, aiming to inspire readers to embark on their own journeys.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Wanderlust Chronicles is to share travel experiences, destination guides, and practical travel tips. The site will provide detailed information on various travel destinations, offer advice on travel planning, and showcase personal stories and photos to inspire others.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What are the top attractions to visit in Paris?

Scenario 2: How can I travel on a budget without compromising the experience?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0056b3 (used for headings and accents)

Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (used for body text and background)

Typography

Heading Font: 'Playfair Display', serif (used for all headings)

Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif (used for all body text)
